Discipline & Conduct
School Rules & Guidelines

BOMIS Purnea maintains a respectful, inclusive, and disciplined environment so every child can thrive. These guidelines reflect our core philosophy of "The Joy of Learning."

General Conduct

All students must wear the prescribed school uniform every working day. Uniform must be clean, pressed, and complete with the school ID card visibly worn.

Students must arrive before the school bell. School hours are 9:00 AM – 3:00 PM. Late arrivals require a written note from a parent or guardian.

Mobile phones are not permitted on school premises during school hours without explicit written permission from the Principal's office.

Respectful behaviour towards teachers, staff, fellow students, and visitors is expected at all times — both inside school and during school-related activities.

Any form of bullying, ragging, or discrimination will be addressed with strict disciplinary action as per CBSE norms and school policy.

Academic Rules

Homework and assignments must be submitted on the due date. Repeated non-submission may result in a parent-teacher meeting.

A minimum of 75% attendance is mandatory to be eligible for term examinations, as per CBSE guidelines.

School diaries / planners must be maintained regularly and signed by parents. They are the primary communication bridge between school and home.

Students must handle school property with care — library books, lab equipment, and furniture. Any damage must be reported to the class teacher immediately.

Attendance & Leave

Leave of absence must be applied in advance via the e-Care Pro app or a written application submitted to the class teacher.

Medical leave beyond 3 consecutive days requires a valid doctor's certificate upon return to school.

Students absent during examinations without prior written notice will not be given a re-test unless truly exceptional circumstances are verified.

Transport & Campus Safety

Students using school buses must board and alight only at their designated stops. Changing stops requires a prior written request to the transport coordinator.

No student may stand in the bus aisle. All students must remain seated with their seat belt fastened throughout the journey.

Students must not leave school premises without written permission. Early departure must be arranged in advance with a parent or authorised guardian pickup.
