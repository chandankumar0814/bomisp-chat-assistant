Discipline & Conduct
School Rules

BOMIS maintains a respectful, inclusive environment so every child can thrive — guided by "The Joy of Learning."

General Conduct

Wear prescribed school uniform every working day — clean, pressed, with school ID card visibly worn.

School hours are 9:00 AM – 3:00 PM. Late arrivals require a written note from parent/guardian.

Mobile phones not permitted during school hours without written permission from the Principal.

Respectful behaviour towards teachers, staff, students, and visitors at all times.

Bullying, ragging, or discrimination will be addressed per CBSE norms and school policy.

Academic Rules

Homework must be submitted on due date. Repeated non-submission leads to parent-teacher meetings.

Minimum 75% attendance required for term exams per CBSE policy.

School diary must be maintained and signed by parents regularly — it's the primary communication tool.

Handle school property with care. Any damage must be reported to the class teacher immediately.

Attendance & Leave

Apply leave via e-Care Pro app or written note submitted to class teacher in advance.

Medical leave over 3 days requires a doctor's certificate on return.

Students absent during exams without prior notice will not receive a re-test without verified exceptional circumstances.

Transport & Safety

Board and alight only at designated bus stops. Stop changes need written request to transport coordinator.

No standing in the bus aisle. All students must remain seated throughout the journey.

No student may leave school premises without written permission or authorised guardian pickup.
